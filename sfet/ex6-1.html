TOKYO REVENGERS

Tokyo Revengers is a Japanese anime and manga series that mixes gang fights, time travel, and emotional drama. It was written and illustrated by Ken Wakui and started as a manga in 2017. The anime adaptation came out in 2021.

PLOT:

The story follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a 26-year-old guy whose life is going nowhere. One day, he learns that his ex-girlfriend Hinata Tachibana and her brother were killed by a gang called the Tokyo Manji Gang (Toman).
