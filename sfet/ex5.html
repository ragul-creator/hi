ABOUT FANDOM HISTORY

ANIME WORLD

The world of anime is vast, colorful, and incredibly diverse, blending art, storytelling, and culture from Japan (and sometimes beyond) into a unique form of entertainment. Anime refers to animated series or films that feature various genres, themes, and art styles. The word “anime” itself is short for the Japanese word animetion, which simply means "animation.

ART STYLE

One of the most distinctive features of anime is its art style. It typically features: Exaggerated facial expressions (big eyes, colorful hair, unique designs). Dynamic action scenes with smooth transitions. Bright and imaginative environments. The art can range from simple and minimalist to highly detailed and hyper-realistic, depending on the tone and style of the anime.
